Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

When you book a session or event through our website, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and payment information. Payment processing is handled securely by Square — we do not store your credit card details on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process bookings, send confirmation and reminder emails via Acuity Scheduling, and communicate about your session. We may also use your email to send occasional updates about our studios.

Analytics

We use PostHog to understand how visitors use our website. PostHog collects anonymous usage data such as pages visited and time on site. No personally identifiable information is shared with PostHog.

Third-Party Services

Our website integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Acuity Scheduling — appointment booking and calendar management
  • Square — secure payment processing
  • PostHog — anonymous website analytics
  • YouTube — video hosting for instructional guides

Each service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies for analytics and to remember your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at contact@whitewallstudios.co.
