You can reschedule or cancel your booking up to 48 hours before your session start time.

To do this, scroll to the bottom of your booking confirmation email and click "Change/Cancel Appointment."

Within 48 hours of your session, bookings can no longer be changed or canceled per the Booking Agreement.

The reschedule/cancellation window closes 48 hours before your session start time.

After that point, changes are no longer allowed according to the terms of the Booking Agreement.

To change your booking length, you will need to cancel your current booking and rebook with the correct number of hours.

After canceling, your refund will typically appear in 3–5 business days.

Once a cancellation is processed, our customer service team will manually issue the refund.
