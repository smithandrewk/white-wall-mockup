Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking and visiting our studios

Both studios are self-service with keypad entry. After booking, you'll receive an email with your unique access code and detailed directions. You can arrive and get started on your own schedule — no staff required.

You may cancel or reschedule up to 48 hours before your session at no charge. Cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable.

Approved refunds are processed within 3-5 business days to your original payment method.
