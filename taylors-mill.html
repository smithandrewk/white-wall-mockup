The Studio That Started It All

Our original WhiteWall studio, located inside the historic Taylors Mill complex.

This 1,000 sq ft space is known for its timeless mill aesthetic, original windows, and authentic character that many photographers are drawn to. It’s a space that keeps things simple—letting the texture, light, and subject take center stage.

Tucked within Taylor’s Mill, the studio offers a unique, creative environment with a short walk through the complex from the main parking area. Shared restroom facilities are located just down the hall, maintaining the original character of the space.

Taylors Mill is a great fit for simple, intentional sessions—portraits, quick shoots, and creatives who prefer a more minimal, organic setting.

For those looking for a more spacious, fully private, and amenity-rich experience, our Powdersville flagship location offers a purpose-built studio designed for comfort, flexibility, and a more elevated production environment.

What’s NOT Included

Private Bathroom

Restroom facilities are located down the hallway and shared within the mill building.

On-Site Parking

Parking is located in the main mill parking lot, followed by a short walk through the complex to reach the studio.

Garage Door Access

This location does not have direct vehicle access, so equipment must be carried into the building.

Large Open Space

At 1,000 sq ft, this studio is best suited for smaller portrait sessions rather than larger productions or events.

Sound Proofing

This location is not designed for sound sensitive shoots, as there is a loud train that goes by often.

Private Studio Facility

Because the studio is located inside the mill complex, shared hallways and building traffic may be present, allowing people to see through the entry door as they walk by.
