Our original WhiteWall location, located inside the historic Taylors Mill complex.

This 1,000 sq ft space is known for its timeless mill aesthetic, original windows, and authentic character that many photographers are drawn to.

Tucked within Taylor’s Mill, the location offers a unique, creative environment with a short walk through the complex from the main parking area.

Taylors Mill is a great fit for simple, intentional sessions—portraits, quick shoots, and creatives who prefer a more minimal, organic setting.

For those looking for a more spacious, fully private, and amenity-rich experience, our Flagship Location offers a purpose-built location designed for comfort, flexibility, and a more elevated production environment.
