The studio that started it all

The original studio that our community has known and loved for over four years with 1,200+ bookings. Beautiful natural light pours through oversized mill windows, creating the perfect canvas for portrait sessions, branding shoots, and headshots.

Located in the historic Taylors Mill complex, this 1,000 sq ft space offers timeless character with 100-year-old mill-style concrete floors, an original cinder block back wall, and original mill-style windows that fill the room with natural light.
