The Location You've Been Looking For

Watch Tour

The Flagship Location is our premier WhiteWall location—purpose-built from the ground up to deliver the most seamless, fully equipped creative and event experience in the Greenville area.

With over 2,000 sq ft of fully private space, this space was designed for photographers, creators, and event hosts who want room to work, complete privacy, and a clean, modern environment that simply works.

Arrive, pull directly up to the 12-ft garage door, unload in seconds, and walk straight into your space.

Natural light fills the space through a massive 40-ft wide by 12-ft tall window wall, creating a soft, high-end look throughout the day. Remote blackout shades and window diffusion give you full control when you need it.

The space also includes a dedicated hair and makeup area, on-site parking just steps from the door, and a private en-suite bathroom.

Inside, the space is built to support real productions with real clients. You’ll have access to large modular set-building cubes, and an expansive collection of furniture and props.

We offer a range of add-ons including seamless paper backdrops, premium lighting kits, TV displays, a PA system, and our signature rolling accent walls with multiple textures.

Planning an event? This location offers 100 chairs, 10 eight-foot tables, and a seamless indoor-outdoor setup.

Located just minutes from downtown Greenville, the Flagship Location was designed for those who want the full WhiteWall experience.

Host Your Event

Our Flagship Location is a flexible, open-concept space that adapts to any gathering. From birthday parties and bridal showers to workshops, fitness classes, and small weddings.

The 12ft garage door makes load-in simple and can open for an indoor-outdoor feel. We have 100 chairs and 10 eight-foot tables available.

The space can be used as-is or cleared entirely into the storage building for a completely empty event space.
