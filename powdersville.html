The Studio You've Been Looking For

The Powdersville location is our flagship WhiteWall studio—purpose-built from the ground up to deliver the most seamless, fully equipped studio and event experience in the Greenville area.

With over 2,000 sq ft of fully private space, this studio was designed for photographers, creators, and event hosts who want room to work, complete privacy, and a clean, modern environment that simply works.

Arrive, pull directly up to the 12-ft garage door, unload in seconds, and walk straight into your space. From the moment you step in, everything is designed to feel smooth, comfortable, and ready to go—so you can stay focused on creating.

Natural light fills the studio through a massive 40-ft wide by 12-ft tall window wall, creating a soft, high-end look throughout the day. Remote blackout shades and window diffusion give you full control when you need it.

The space also includes a dedicated hair and makeup area, on-site parking just steps from the door, and a private en-suite bathroom—adding to the overall comfort and ease of the experience.

Inside, the studio is built to support real productions with real clients. You’ll have access to large modular set-building cubes, and an expansive collection of furniture and props—giving you the flexibility to shape the space however you need.

Our studio offers a range of add-ons to further customize your setup, including seamless paper backdrops, premium lighting kits, TV displays, a PA system, and our signature rolling accent walls with multiple textures—allowing you to quickly transform the space and create a wide range of looks, all within a single booking.

Planning an event? This location offers 100 chairs, 10 eight-foot tables, and a seamless indoor-outdoor setup.

Located just minutes from downtown Greenville, the Powdersville studio was designed for those who want the full WhiteWall experience—a space that feels polished, comfortable, and incredibly easy to work in, whether you’re shooting, producing, or hosting.

Event Space

Host your next event

Our Powdersville location is designed to be a flexible, open-concept space that can easily adapt to whatever type of gathering you have in mind. From birthday parties, bridal showers and workshops, to fitness classes, pop-ups, and small weddings, the layout allows you to shape the space exactly how you want it.

The studio can be used as-is with the existing furniture and props, or you can clear the space entirely. Anything in the studio can simply be moved into the storage building located directly in front of the garage door, giving you the option of a completely empty event space if needed.

The 12ft garage door makes load-in simple for caterers, vendors, and event setup, and it can also be opened to create an indoor-outdoor feel using the concrete area just outside the studio. When you book the space for an event, the surrounding parking area and exterior space are also available for your use.

We also have 100 chairs and 10 eight-foot tables available to add to your booking, making it easy to accommodate larger gatherings.

If you’d like to host an event with us, simply complete the booking form and reserve the time slot that works best for you. Be sure to include enough time in your reservation for both setup and teardown. Most events can be booked and confirmed entirely through the self-service process, and you’re typically all set once your reservation is complete.

For gatherings larger than 30 guests, we may reach out to confirm a few details. The space can accommodate up to 150 people depending on the setup.
